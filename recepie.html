Creamy Garlic Pasta

A rich and comforting pasta dish made with a silky garlic cream sauce, fresh herbs, and parmesan cheese. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 25 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 250g spaghetti or fettuccine
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Optional: red chili flakes for extra heat

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta
    Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve ½ cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Prepare the garlic base
    Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Make the sauce
    Pour in the heavy cream and stir gently. Add oregano, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Add the cheese
    Stir in the parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. If the sauce becomes too thick, add a little reserved pasta water.
  5. Combine
    Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss until every strand is coated in the creamy sauce.
  6. Serve
    Garnish with fresh parsley and a sprinkle of chili flakes if desired. Serve immediately while hot.
